About CCLS

Who we are and what drives us at the Corporate & Commercial Law Society

Learn More About Us

The Corporate & Commercial Law Society (CCLS) at Campus Law Centre, Faculty of Law, University of Delhi is a student-driven academic initiative dedicated to fostering awareness, research, and professional engagement in the field of corporate and commercial law.

The Society was established with the vision of bridging the gap between theoretical legal education and the practical realities of the corporate legal profession, enabling students to develop both strong legal foundations and industry-relevant skills.

CCLS provides a dynamic platform for students to explore diverse areas of corporate law through lectures, seminars, workshops, training sessions, and conferences.
